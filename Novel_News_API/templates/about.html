About Novel News

We are members of CS 411 Fall 2018. We have created this news app as a one-stop spot for all different news sources. We believe that millenials are less informed about current events than they should be and we feel that some of this can be attributed to the way news is communicated. By connecting social media and youtube to top stories, we are showing young people how easy it is to stay up to date using apps they use every day!
